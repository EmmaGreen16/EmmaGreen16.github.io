The Lich

The Lich is a creature in Adventure Time that is older than existance itself. A survivor of a time before there was nothing. He is a ancient cosmic symbol of death.

  • The lich became a physical being near the end of
    the mushroom war from the mutagenic bomb.
  • The lichs main goal is to eradocate all life in the multiverse until there is nothing but himself remains.
  • The Lich has done everything to try to make his goal come true. In one reality it did come true and he was distraught about it.
  • The Lich is not interested in fame, power, or
    dominion over others he only desires to wipe out all life from existence.
  • The Lich kills Prismo the
    Wishmaster.
  • The Lich gets turned into
    sweet pea because he touched a magical goo that heals wounds. This changed him to a sweet boy named Sweet Pea.

  1. The Lich has the power of
    Necromancy.
  2. The Lich has the power of Energy Manipulation.
  3. The Lich has the power of immortality.
  4. The Lich has the power of Mind Control.
  5. The Lich has the power of control over Death
