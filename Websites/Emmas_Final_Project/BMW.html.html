About BMW

BMW, short for Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a globally renowned German automobile and motorcycle manufacturer known for blending luxury, performance, and engineering precision. Founded in 1916, BMW originally produced aircraft engines before transitioning to motorcycles and later to automobiles. The brand has built a strong identity around driving dynamics, with its slogan “The Ultimate Driving Machine” reflecting its focus on delivering engaging and responsive vehicles. From its sporty sedans like the 3 Series to its high-performance M models and innovative electric vehicles under the BMW i line, the company has maintained a strong reputation for quality.
