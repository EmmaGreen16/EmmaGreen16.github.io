About Toyota

Toyota is a globally respected Japanese automotive manufacturer known for its reliability, efficiency, and innovation. Founded in 1937, Toyota has grown to become one of the largest carmakers in the world, producing a wide range of vehicles from practical sedans and SUVs to performance cars and hybrids. The brand is especially praised for its long-lasting vehicles, low maintenance costs, and commitment to quality. Toyota also led the way in hybrid technology with the launch of the Prius, the worlds first mass-produced hybrid car. In recent years, the company has expanded its efforts in sustainability with electric and hydrogen fuel cell vehicles.
