How We Rave

Raving is feeling the music, dancing and moving with the music. Most people headbang to the music. Other ways people dance to EDM is jumping, stomping, and even fist pumping.

Why We Do It

Raving is an art. We believe in PLUR to keep the rave atmosphere peaceful and calm. Plur means Peace, Love, Unity and Respect.
